Pet portraits in Portland, OR

Pepper and Lark is a one-woman, one-dog photography team bringing professional and warm portraits of animals (and the people who love them) to the larger Portland, Oregon area. Whether it's in any of the area's gorgeous parks and trails, the comfort of their own home, or featuring animals at one of Portland's many fantastic rescue organizations, I love connecting with and photographing creatures of all shapes and sizes. My passion is bringing out the individual personality of each animal, creating images that will be cherished for a lifetime.
